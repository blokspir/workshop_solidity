# Module 01: Blockchain and Solana Fundamentals

**Estimated Time:** 3-4 hours
**Prerequisites:** Module 00 (environment setup complete)

## Overview

This module takes you from zero blockchain knowledge to a solid understanding of how Solana works. You'll learn what blockchains are, why Solana is different, and the mental models you need before writing any code.

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this module, you will be able to:

1. Explain what a blockchain is and why it matters
2. Describe how Solana differs from other blockchains
3. Understand Solana's account model (the single most important concept)
4. Explain what programs, transactions, and instructions are
5. Understand PDAs and why they exist
6. Navigate Solana Explorer and read on-chain data
7. Distinguish between SOL and SPL tokens
